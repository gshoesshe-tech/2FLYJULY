Cash accounts

How to read this

New product cash is money newly added to cash accounts this month.

Migrated paid orders were already included in opening cash, so they are not added to GCash again.

Recognized costed sales is the paid portion of new costed inventory already committed to packing.

Legacy recovery is cash from old stock with unknown cost and is excluded from automatic owner pay.

Ongoing production paid value is money already inside unfinished production.

Open payables are amounts still owed to sewers, cutters, suppliers, or subcons.

Inbound delivery cost added is the Lalamove cost attached to materials, production, or finished inventory.

Inbound delivery cash paid is the portion that actually left your cash accounts this month. Trips marked paid before system do not reduce cash again.

Equipment/assets reduce cash but are not mixed with normal operating expenses.
